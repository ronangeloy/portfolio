BBX AU

BBX stands for Business to Business Exchange, a global trade network that allows businesses to trade goods and services within a trusted community. Founded in 1993, BBX has grown from a national trade exchange to a global network that has facilitated millions of business transactions across multiple countries. It has continuously evolved to incorporate digital technology, making trade easier and more accessible for businesses worldwide.

Brackish

Brackish Inc. prides itself on being at the forefront of sustainable practices within the aquaculture industry. Their commitment to green aquaculture is evident in every aspect of their operations, from the selection of sites for their farms to the methods used to raise and harvest fish. By focusing on creating a minimal environmental footprint, Brackish Inc. ensures that its activities contribute positively to the health of the planet. This approach includes the use of renewable energy sources, the implementation of water recycling systems, and the development of feed that does not deplete wild fish stocks, showcasing their dedication to sustainability.
